our solution

Our mobile app leverages on alternative data analytics, social networks and blockchain to empower informal trusted networks access to affordable financial services.

Quicker underwriting and processing of loans for anyone anywhere.
Support of multiple currencies.
Use of multiple non-traditional alternative data sources for credit scoring.
Use of affinity networks for financial identity verification through blockchain technology.

our impact

We are on a mission to bring hope to the undeserved and excluded population by using alternative behavioral data to build credit scores then connect them with affordable access to credit for wealth creation opportunities.
